Lasagna

Description

A typical italian dish, more precisely from Bologna

It is made with pasta, ragù and besciamella

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Parmigiano

Steps

  1. Make the pasta
  2. Make the ragù
  3. Make the besciamella
  4. Combine everything and add parmigiano
  5. Put in the oven
